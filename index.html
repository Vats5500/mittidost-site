🌱 The Problem

Rural farmers lack access to soil testing due to distance, low awareness, and cost. This leads to poor fertilizer use and low crop productivity.

🚀 Our Solution

MittiDost provides free soil testing through village-level youth (MittiMitras), ensuring last-mile delivery of agriculture services and schemes.

⚙️ How It Works

  • Farmer connects with MittiMitra
  • Soil sample collected
  • Sent to lab
  • Report + guidance delivered

💡 Why MittiDost

  • Village-level trusted network
  • Free for farmers
  • Improves yield & income
  • Bridges gap with government schemes

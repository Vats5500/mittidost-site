Building the Last-Mile Infrastructure for Indian Farmers

MittiDost delivers free soil testing through village-level networks, improving crop yield and farmer income.

Join Pilot

🌱 The Problem

Rural farmers lack access to soil testing due to distance, low awareness, and cost. This leads to incorrect fertilizer use and lower crop productivity.

🚀 Our Solution

MittiDost provides free soil testing through trained rural youth (MittiMitras), ensuring last-mile delivery of agriculture services and government schemes.

⚙️ How It Works

  • Farmer connects with MittiMitra
  • Soil sample collected from farm
  • Sample tested in government lab
  • Report and guidance delivered

💡 Why MittiDost

  • Village-level trusted network
  • Free for farmers via government support
  • Improves crop yield and farmer income
  • Bridges gap between farmers and schemes

📊 Why Now?

Government is actively promoting soil health programs, but last-mile delivery is missing. MittiDost bridges this gap with a scalable rural network.

🌾 Targeting 14+ Crore Farmers Across India

🌱 The Problem

Rural farmers lack access to soil testing due to distance, low awareness, and cost barriers. This results in incorrect fertilizer use, reduced crop yield, and income loss.

🚀 Our Solution

MittiDost deploys trained rural youth (MittiMitras) to collect soil samples, facilitate government testing, and deliver simple, actionable soil insights to farmers.

⚙️ How It Works

📍 Connect with MittiMitra
🧪 Soil sample collected
🏢 Tested in govt lab
📄 Report + guidance delivered

💡 Why MittiDost

  • Trusted village-level human network
  • Free for farmers via government support
  • Improves yield and reduces input cost
  • Bridges farmers with schemes

📊 Why Now?

Government is actively promoting soil health programs, but last-mile delivery remains weak. MittiDost solves this gap with a scalable rural execution network.

🌱 The Problem

Millions of farmers in India still rely on guesswork for fertilizer usage due to lack of access to soil testing facilities. Government schemes exist, but fail at last-mile delivery — leaving farmers underserved and facing reduced yields.

🚀 Our Solution

MittiDost builds a decentralized network of trained rural youth (MittiMitras) who collect soil samples, facilitate testing through government labs, and deliver actionable insights directly to farmers at zero cost.

📊 Market Opportunity

India has over 14 crore farmers, with more than 85% being small and marginal. Over 90% of villages lack soil testing access, creating a massive scalable opportunity.

📊 Why Now

Government is actively promoting soil health programs, but execution remains weak. This creates the perfect opportunity for MittiDost to build the last-mile network.

🌍 Our Impact Vision

Improve farmer income, increase productivity, reduce input waste, and create rural employment at scale.
